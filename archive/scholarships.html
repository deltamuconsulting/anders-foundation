Anders Foundation Scholarships

Every year The Foundation will award financial grants of up to £1000 to promising disabled young (under 20 players) in categories that are likely to be recognized in due course as acceptable for participation in the Paralympics such as Wheelchair, Dwarf and Leg amputees in the hope of future Gold Medals in Paralympic Badminton.


Badminton England and current GB Parabadminton player Bobby Griffin, who has been integral in establishing this fund, will advise The Foundation on selecting the recipients of this award and provide proof of legitimate bills to be paid such as equipment, travel, coaching etc.

Registered UK Charity 1149870.

The trustees are Heather Nielsen, Bjorn Nielsen, Gorm Nielsen and Nick Ponting.
