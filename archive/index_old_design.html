The Anders Foundation was formed as a legacy to Anders Nielsen who sadly lost his life in 2010 after a year-long fight with cancer.


To celebrate Anders' determination to succeed and admirable work ethic, highlighted by the international success he achieved in his Badminton career, we aim to support young people of all abilities, both disabled and able bodied, to achieve their Badminton goals.


The Anders Foundation will award monies throughout the UK to individuals and clubs / institutions on a case by case basis considered by the trustees and in conjunction with selected experts from within the sport of badminton.


Anders Nielsen Golf Day 17/06/13

The Anders Nielsen Golf Day is held to support the Anders Nielsen Foundation and Sparks charity.


Held at the exclusive members-only Royal Automobile Club. Sweeping down towards Epsom racecourse, the tree-lined grounds make for a dramatic backdrop to a round of golf at any time of year. It offers the perfect setting for this wonderful golf day.


The tournament is a team competition - Fourball Betterball Stableford (best two of four scores to count). Handicap allowance: Men 24, Ladies 36.
